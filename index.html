CapMyBeast

CapMyBeast is a minimalist, visual budgeting tool that helps you tame your emotional spending by setting a weekly spending cap. Choose a companion Beast and track your progress with a dynamic fill bar that shows how close you are to your limit.

  • 🐉 Set your weekly budget cap
  • 📊 Log your spending once per week
  • 💥 Your Beast reacts visually — happy if you're under, droopy if you overspend
  • 🔥 Track your under-cap streaks over time

This is a 100% offline-first, privacy-safe budgeting app — no logins, no syncing. Just you, your wallet, and your Beast.
